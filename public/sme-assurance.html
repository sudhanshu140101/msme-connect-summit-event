Ageas Federal Life Insurance

🏢 About the Company

Ageas Federal Life Insurance is one of India’s leading private life insurers and operates as a joint venture between Ageas, a multinational insurance group based in Europe, and Federal Bank, one of India’s leading private sector banks. Since commencing operations in 2008, the company achieved breakeven within five years, driven by its strong focus on innovation, customer-centricity, and sustainable growth. Through a nationwide distribution network that includes partner bank branches, advisors, and digital channels, the company has established a strong presence across India.

💼 Products and Business Scale

The company offers a diverse portfolio of wealth management, protection, savings, and retirement solutions for both individual and corporate customers. Flagship offerings include Wealth Gain Insurance Plan, Super Protect Plan, and Magic Savings Plan designed to support long-term wealth creation, protection, and guaranteed savings needs across different life stages.
