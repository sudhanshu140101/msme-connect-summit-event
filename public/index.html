CIMSME

Chamber of Indian Micro Small & Medium Enterprises

Mukesh Mohan Gupta is a President of CIMSME and a Chartered Accountant by Profession having more than 35 years of experience in Banking, Finance, MSME, Economy, Law Accounting and Auditing.

Empowering MSMEs

Promoting & Protecting Trade & Commerce

MSMEs

Growth

🎯

Support

"CIMSME" prevails to promote & protect the trade & commerce of MSMEs in India. The MSME sector plays a very important role in overall economic growth of India.

Understanding the importance of SME sector, CIMSME has taken proactive steps to spread awareness among the MSMEs for their conclusive growth by sharing the ideas and expertise.

Non-Profit Organization

CIMSME is a Non-profit organization which represents interest of enterprises, particularly in MSME sector, with Banks, Financial Institutions, concerned Ministries and other Stakeholders, to promote bilateral dialogue for the benefit of MSMEs.
