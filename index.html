About CIMSME

Chamber of Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (CIMSME) is a key industry Chamber dedicated to the empowering and wellbeing of MSMEs & Startups through guidance, mentorship, skill development programs & financial literacy. At CIMSME, we are committed to create a prospering ecosystem for startups and MSMEs in India.

Guidance & Mentorship Support for entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey
Financial Literacy Helping MSMEs access schemes, funding, and growth tools
Policy Advocacy Creating a stronger, more connected MSME ecosystem in India

Frequently Asked Questions

Join MSME Connect Summit - 2026, a powerful platform designed to help MSMEs, entrepreneurs, and professionals understand and access government schemes, financial support, and growth opportunities for their businesses.

This event is ideal for MSME Business Owners, Entrepreneurs & Startup Founders, Chartered Accountants and Consultants, Traders, Manufacturers, and Service Providers, Financial Advisors, Professionals working with MSMEs, and anyone interested in government schemes and MSME funding opportunities.

Participants will gain insights into Collateral-Free Loans up to ₹20 Crores, Self-Reliant Fund support for MSMEs, TReDS Invoice Discounting, IPR Reimbursement schemes, GeM Registration process, Digital Marketing through Government schemes, Government & Private Tender opportunities, and Solutions for MSME delayed payments.

Date: 27 June 2026. Time: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM (With Networking Lunch). Location: Lodhi Road, New Delhi. Language: Hindi & English.

The registration fee for the event is ₹2,360 (Inclusive of GST).

Yes. Participants will have the opportunity to interact with industry experts, financial institutions, and MSME advisors during the session and networking opportunities.

The event is organized by the Chamber of Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (CIMSME), an organization committed to supporting MSMEs through financial awareness, policy advocacy, and business development initiatives.
